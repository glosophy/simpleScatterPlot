Horsepower and miles per galon are negatively correlated. Cars with 8 cylinders seem to be the ones that perform better in terms of fuel consumed per mile, whereas those with 4 cylinders are usually the ones that consume more fuel per mile show a decrease in their horsepower. 6-cylinder cars make up the average of the last two. The odd-one-outs: cars with 5 cylinders and 3 cylinders.
